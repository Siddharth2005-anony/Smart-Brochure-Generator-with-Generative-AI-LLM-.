**YANGWANG U9 Xtreme**
*The Pinnacle of Electric Hypercar Performance*

**Introduction**
Experience the future of speed and luxury with the YANGWANG U9 Xtreme, the world’s fastest production car. Born from cutting-edge innovation and advanced engineering by BYD’s luxury brand YANGWANG, the U9 Xtreme shatters records with a top speed of 496 km/h (308 mph), combining unprecedented electric power with sustainable performance[2][7].

**Key Features**
- **Unrivalled Power:** Four ultra-high RPM electric motors deliver an astonishing combined output of nearly 3,000 PS (approximately 2,978 hp), achieving a power-to-weight ratio of 1,217 hp per tonne[1][3][11].
- **Advanced Battery System:** Equipped with a 1200V ultra-high voltage platform and a lithium iron phosphate Blade Battery enabling a 30C discharge rate, offering rapid energy delivery and improved thermal management[2][5].
- **High-Performance Aerodynamics:** Extensive carbon fiber components, fixed rear wing, enlarged rear diffuser, and optimized air intakes and side skirts improve ground effects and stability at extreme speeds[1].
- **Track-Level Suspension & Brakes:** Featuring DiSus-X intelligent body control suspension with aviation-grade aluminium and titanium brake calipers paired with carbon ceramic discs for superior handling and stopping power[1].
- **Specialized Tires:** Custom-designed ultra-high-speed tyres with aramid fiber belts and enhanced grip compounds withstand velocities exceeding 500 km/h[1].
- **Record-Breaking Performance:** Holds official records including the Nürburgring Nordschleife lap under 7 minutes and the highest top speed for a production electric car globally[1][8][12].
